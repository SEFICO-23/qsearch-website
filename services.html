Every Business is Different

Your security strategy,
built around your reality

No two organizations face the same threats. A SaaS platform has different attack surfaces than a financial institution. A gambling operator faces different regulatory pressure than a healthcare provider. A fiduciary handles different sensitive data than an e-commerce business.

After your free assessment, we build a tailored engagement around your specific technology stack, regulatory landscape, and business context. The scope adapts. The depth doesn't.

  • 01
    Technology analysis — we map your stack, frameworks, APIs, cloud providers, and third-party integrations to identify the attack surfaces specific to your architecture.
  • 02
    Regulatory mapping — nDSG, GDPR, FINMA, MGA, UKGC, PCI DSS, HIPAA — we align testing to the compliance frameworks that apply to your industry.
  • 03
    Threat modeling — we identify who would attack you, why, and how — then test those specific scenarios with proven exploitation techniques.
  • 04
    Business impact — every finding is quantified in your currency and your context. Not abstract risk scores — real financial and operational exposure.
