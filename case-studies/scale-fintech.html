The Engagement

A no-constraints adversarial simulation, fully authorized

A scale-stage online financial platform requested a comprehensive, no-constraints security assessment. The engagement covered web application security, infrastructure, OSINT, social engineering, and credential testing with full authorization for destructive testing.

QuantumSearch deployed 26 specialized actors across 8 escalating phases over a 24-hour period, progressing from passive reconnaissance through active exploitation to advanced credential testing and supply chain analysis.

What We Found

36 unique findings. Here's what broke.

8Critical
14High
8Medium
6Low / Info

Plus 2 business and regulatory observations. All findings passed through three quality gates before delivery.

Critical 01 Three Independent Paths to Unauthorized Financial Manipulation Critical

Each proven with working proof-of-concept. The platform's API layer permitted unauthenticated callers to modify account balances, inject transaction records, and manipulate financial state through multiple independent attack vectors.

Critical 02 Complete Outcome Prediction Critical

The platform's fairness mechanism returned cryptographic seeds in plaintext, allowing any observer to predict every outcome. This finding alone would constitute a regulatory catastrophe for a licensed platform.

Critical 03 Full Email Domain Impersonation Critical

8 out of 8 spoofed emails delivered successfully across 7 distinct sender personas on the platform's domain. No DMARC, DKIM, or SPF enforcement. Remediation cost: $0 (DNS configuration, 30 minutes).

Critical 04 Unrestricted API Access Critical

The platform's API gateway exposed 87 endpoints across 56 database tables to any caller with the API key embedded in the frontend JavaScript. Sensitive data including game economics, user financial records, and operational configuration readable without authentication.

What Held (Equally Important)

Honest reporting documents what worked as clearly as what broke

All credentials survived brute-force testing. JWT secret, database password, SSH credentials, and admin panel login all withstood 14.3 million+ password attempts including the full rockyou.txt wordlist. Strong credential management is explicitly documented as a positive finding.

Row-Level Security on sensitive tables blocked all 14 bypass techniques tested. The database's access control on configuration tables held against UPSERT bypass, schema switching, SQL injection via filters, and 11 other documented techniques.

Methodology

8 phases. 26 actors. Quality-gated findings.

  • 8-phase escalation: passive recon, initial exploitation, deep exploitation, game/platform exploitation, advanced tools (nuclei, ffuf, testssl), CVE-based escalation, multi-vector final sweep, deep credential research
  • 26 specialized actors, each with defined scope and kill criteria
  • All findings verified from hardened infrastructure (VPN + isolated environment)
  • Every finding includes working proof-of-concept or documented reproduction
  • Quality pipeline: Judge + Devil's Advocate review (parallel), then independent QA gate
  • Findings that failed quality gates were killed, not downgraded
Business Impact

Three dimensions of existential risk

For a financial platform preparing to process real transactions, the findings represent existential risk across three dimensions:

Financial

Unlimited unauthorized balance manipulation creates direct monetary exposure the moment withdrawal processing goes live.

Regulatory

Predictable outcomes and exposed financial data violate licensing requirements in every jurisdiction where the platform intended to operate.

Reputational

Email domain impersonation enables targeted phishing campaigns impersonating the platform's own staff, eroding user trust before the platform even launches publicly.

Outcome

45-page bilingual audit report, three quality gates passed

  • Full audit report delivered (45 pages, bilingual English/Italian)
  • All findings passed three quality gates (Judge + Devil's Advocate, Business Impact Specialist, independent QA)
  • Engagement demonstrated QuantumSearch's capacity for comprehensive, multi-phase assessments with 26 parallel actors coordinated across 8 phases
