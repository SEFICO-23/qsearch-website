Our Story

Why QuantumSearch exists

We started as security researchers. Not consultants — researchers. The kind who spend nights tracing authentication flows, timing race conditions, and chaining low-severity bugs into critical exploits. We discovered hundreds of vulnerabilities in production systems used by millions.

Along the way, we saw the same pattern everywhere: companies paying for security assessments that amounted to automated scans wrapped in expensive PDFs. Hundreds of "findings" — mostly noise — while the real threats hid in business logic, timing windows, and trust boundaries that no scanner understands.

We built QuantumSearch to close that gap. Based in Ticino, Switzerland, we combine AI-driven reconnaissance with the manual depth that only comes from active research. Every vulnerability we report is proven exploitable, quantified in business impact, and accompanied by clear remediation.

Core Values

How we operate

Six principles that guide every engagement, every finding, every interaction.

Proof Over Theory

Every critical finding includes a working proof-of-concept. We don't submit theoretical observations — we prove exploitability in controlled environments.

Impact Over Volume

We don't inflate reports with low-severity noise to look thorough. We focus on findings that actually put your business at risk and quantify them in your currency.

Partnership Over Transaction

We don't deliver a report and disappear. Remediation guidance, verification retesting, and ongoing advisory are part of every relationship.

Swiss Precision

Operating under Swiss law, compliant with nDSG and GDPR. We understand the regulatory frameworks that affect our clients — FINMA, BGS, MGA, UKGC.

Absolute Confidentiality

Client data never leaves our secure environment. Engagement details are protected by NDA and Swiss confidentiality standards. No exceptions.

Attacker Mindset, Defender Purpose

We think like attackers to serve defenders. Active bug bounty researchers who discover real vulnerabilities daily — that experience directly benefits every client.

Our Methodology

Three phases.
Zero assumptions.

Every engagement follows a structured methodology that combines AI breadth with human depth.

01

Discover

AI-powered reconnaissance maps your full attack surface — subdomains, APIs, cloud services, third-party integrations, and supply chain dependencies.

02

Exploit
