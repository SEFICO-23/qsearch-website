# QuantumSearch — Influencer & Partnership Outreach

**Market:** Italy + Ticino (CH)
**Language:** Italiano (outreach) + English (tech community)
**Objective:** Brand credibility, technical authority, B2C signups
**Date:** 2026-04-10

---

## Market Reality

The Italian cybersecurity influencer ecosystem is **academic and professional**, not creator-driven. There are no major Italian cybersecurity YouTubers with 100K+ subscribers. The community builds authority through LinkedIn, conference speaking, academic research, and open-source tools.

**This is an advantage for QuantumSearch.** The market is underserved by content creators — a partnership with even a mid-tier figure carries outsized credibility. And the professional/academic angle aligns perfectly with our B2B positioning.

**Strategy:** Mix of (1) cybersecurity researchers/professors for credibility, (2) Italian tech/business influencers for reach, and (3) micro-influencers in the IT community for grassroots adoption.

## Affiliate Commission Structure

### Standard Affiliate Program

| Tier | Commission | Who |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Standard | 15% recurring (12 months) | Anyone with a referral link |
| Influencer | 20% recurring (12 months) | Verified influencers with 5K+ following |
| Premium Partner | 25% recurring (lifetime) | Top 5 performers or strategic partners |
| Academic | 0% (free accounts instead) | Universities and training programs |

### How It Works

1. Influencer gets a unique referral link: `qsearch.ch/?ref=NOME`
2. Visitor signs up → cookie tracks attribution (30-day window)
3. When visitor upgrades to paid plan (Plus/Pro/Max), influencer earns commission
4. Commission paid monthly via bank transfer or PayPal
5. Dashboard shows: clicks, signups, conversions, earnings

### Revenue Examples

| Scenario | Monthly Signups via Link | Avg. Plan | Monthly Commission (20%) |

| Top performer | 40 paid users | Mix (CHF 49 avg) | CHF 392 |

### Follow-Up Rules

1. **Wait 5 business days** before first follow-up
2. **Maximum 2 follow-ups** — never chase. If they don't respond after 2, move on.
3. **Never send identical messages** to multiple people — personalize every outreach
4. **Track all outreach** in a spreadsheet: name, date sent, channel, response, status
5. **Sign everything as** "QuantumSearch Security Research" — never personal names
